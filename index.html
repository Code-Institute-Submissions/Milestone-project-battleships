Instructions

Welcome to Battleships!

Battleships is a turn-based strategy game in which two opposing players attempt to 'sink' the other's ships before their own are sunk.

Begin by placing your ships onto your board. You are in control of five ships of differing lengths: Aircraft Carrier, Battleship, Submarine, Destroyer and Patrol Boat. You place your ships horizontally or vertically on your board. Simply click the ship you wish to place from the list in the armory, and click the square where you wish to place it. Change the orientation, if you wish, by clicking on the arrow located beneath the armory. Once all your ships have been placed, the game can begin.

Once the game begins, you and your opponent take turns to guess the location of each other's ships. If you correctly guess all the squares where a ship has been placed, that ship has sunk. Sink all your opponent's ships before they sink yours.

Good luck!
